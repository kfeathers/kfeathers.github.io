about

me in a nut shell, well a large nut shell

I am a Georgia peach, born and raised. Growing up, I was an artist with an obsession for taking apart electronics. Looking back, it makes perfect sense that my life would unfurl like it has so far.

I got a BFA with a speciality in Graphic Design. As I started my career as a graphic designer, I got interested in the layer beneath my designs. I started code problem-solving and I was hooked. Hungry to learn more, I devoured course after course online. A self-taught coder, I learned how to research, synthesize information in the moment, and retain each new capability with the assurance of hands-on experience.

Outside of the digital world, I was a dog trainer while I was in school. I won gold in women's boxing at the Georgia Golden Gloves Championship and silver at the National Golden Gloves Championship. And my favorite movies are Labyrinth and Caddyshack...so I got that going for me, which is nice. Reach out, let's connect.
